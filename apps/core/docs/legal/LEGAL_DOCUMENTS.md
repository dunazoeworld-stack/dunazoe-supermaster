# DOCUMENT 1: THRIFT (AJO) SAVINGS TERMS & CONDITIONS

**Service Provider:** DUNAZOE Platform
**Contact:** Dunazoeworld@gmail.com | dunazoe.com

## 1. Service Description
DUNAZOE Ajo Savings is a platform-based thrift savings service that enables users to save money in regular instalments toward personal financial goals and product/service purchases.

## 2. Account Opening
- Any verified DUNAZOE user may open an Ajo account free of charge.
- Account opening requires minimum KYC Level 1 (phone + BVN/NIN verification).
- One primary Ajo account per user. Multiple product-linked sub-accounts permitted.

## 3. Contribution Schedules
- Users may save daily, weekly, biweekly, or monthly.
- Maximum personal savings plan duration: **12 months**.
- Minimum contribution: ₦1,000 per transaction.
- Contributions may come from: wallet, bank transfer, card payment, or third-party funding.

## 4. Interest
- DUNAZOE pays **5% per annum** interest on completed savings cycles.
- Interest is calculated on the average daily balance.
- Interest is credited at end of each completed cycle or annually, whichever is earlier.

## 5. Ajo Surcharge
- Products purchased using Ajo plans with contribution schedules exceeding **14 days (2 weeks)** may attract an additional **10% surcharge** on the product price.
- This surcharge compensates for the extended payment schedule.
- The surcharge amount will be **clearly disclosed** before plan creation.
- The surcharge is policy-configurable and may be waived for select products.

## 6. Withdrawal Rules
- Personal savings may be withdrawn at any time subject to:
  - 48-hour processing period for bank transfers.
  - Funds sent only to verified registered bank accounts.
  - Cooling-off period (48 hours) after adding a new bank account.
- Early withdrawal from product-linked plans may forfeit accumulated interest.

## 7. MANDATORY LOAN RULE
**Maximum loan amount = Total amount contributed.**
- Contributed ₦50,000 → Maximum loan = ₦50,000.
- Contributed ₦120,000 → Maximum loan = ₦120,000.
- This rule is absolute and enforced at the system level.
- No exceptions. No overrides.

## 8. 2% Financial Service Charge
- Where a loan amount equals or exceeds **90% of total contributed amount**, a **2% financial service charge** applies to the loan amount.
- Example: Loan ₦95,000 against ₦100,000 contribution → Charge = ₦1,900.
- This charge is disclosed before loan disbursement and recorded in your ledger.

## 9. Funds Safety
- All Ajo funds are held in segregated accounts separate from DUNAZOE operating funds.
- Funds are not used for DUNAZOE operations or investments without user consent.

# DOCUMENT 2: LOAN TERMS & CONDITIONS

## 1. Eligibility
Loan applications are evaluated on:
- Trust Score (minimum score 40 required)
- Total contributions made
- Contribution consistency
- Repayment history
- Active fraud risk assessment

## 2. Loan Limits
| Trust Tier | Score Range | Multiplier | Max Loan Cap |
|---|---|---|---|
| Platinum | 90–100 | 3× | = Total contributed |
| Gold | 75–89 | 2× | = Total contributed |
| Silver | 60–74 | 1× | = Total contributed |
| Bronze | 40–59 | 0.5× | = Total contributed |
| New | 0–39 | Ineligible | N/A |

**ABSOLUTE RULE: Maximum loan amount NEVER exceeds total contributed amount regardless of trust tier multiplier.**

## 3. Interest Rate
- Flat interest rate: **5% of loan amount** for the agreed repayment period.
- Example: ₦50,000 loan → Total repayable = ₦52,500.

## 4. Repayment
- Repayment period: 7 to 180 days (user's choice at application).
- Repayment may be made from: Ajo savings balance, wallet, or direct payment.
- Early repayment is permitted at no penalty.

## 5. Default & Consequences
- Loans overdue by more than 14 days are considered in default.
- Default reduces Trust Score significantly.
- Defaulted loans reduce future loan eligibility.
- DUNAZOE reserves the right to deduct overdue amounts from Ajo balance.

## 6. Disclosure
All loan terms, charges, and conditions are disclosed before disbursement. No hidden fees.

# DOCUMENT 3: DISPUTE POLICY

## 1. Eligible Disputes
Users may raise disputes for:
- Product not received
- Product not as described
- Service not delivered as agreed
- Delivery damage
- Unauthorized charges
- Refund not received

## 2. Dispute Process
1. Buyer raises dispute within **7 days** of delivery or expected delivery date.
2. Escrow funds are **immediately locked** upon dispute creation.
3. Both parties have **24 hours** to submit evidence.
4. DUNAZOE Support reviews evidence within **48 working hours**.
5. Decision is communicated to both parties.
6. Funds released or refunded within **24 hours** of decision.

## 3. Evidence Requirements
- Photos of received item (if applicable)
- Screenshots of product listing at time of purchase
- Chat messages between buyer and vendor
- Delivery tracking information

## 4. Possible Outcomes
- **Buyer wins:** Full refund from escrow to buyer's wallet.
- **Vendor wins:** Escrow released to vendor's wallet.
- **Split decision:** Partial refund as determined by Support.

## 5. Appeal
Decisions may be appealed once within 48 hours of notification. CEO-level review available for disputes > ₦100,000.

## 6. Platform Charges During Dispute
- Platform fees (5% customer + 5% vendor) are non-refundable unless vendor is found at fault for product misrepresentation.

# DOCUMENT 4: VENDOR AGREEMENT

## 1. Vendor Eligibility
All vendors must:
- Complete KYC Level 2 (government ID + selfie)
- Provide valid business information
- Have at least one verified bank account registered
- Accept these terms and all DUNAZOE policies

## 2. Vendor Types
- **Direct Vendor:** Lists and sells own products/services
- **Copytrader Vendor:** Copies approved products with 6% markup
- **Delivery Vendor / Pickup Station:** Handles deliveries and pickups
- **Hybrid Vendor:** Combination of above

## 3. Platform Charges
- **5% system charge** deducted from vendor's side of every transaction.
- **5% system charge** charged to customer's side of every transaction.
- **2% delivery commission** paid to delivery vendors for inter-location deliveries.
- **₦5,000 milestone bonus** paid to delivery agents for every 100 eligible completed deliveries.
- Charges are transparent and disclosed on every transaction receipt.

## 4. Copytrading Rules
- Only approved products may be copied.
- Minimum markup: 6% above original vendor price.
- Self-referral copying is strictly prohibited.
- Chain copying (copying a copy) is prohibited.
- DUNAZOE reserves the right to limit copytrader ratios.

## 5. Payout Schedule
- Payouts are processed **every 14 days (biweekly)**.
- Funds sent only to verified registered bank accounts.
- Minimum payout: ₦5,000.
- Payout statements available in vendor dashboard.

## 6. No Cash Policy
**DUNAZOE strictly prohibits cash on delivery.**
All transactions must be processed through the DUNAZOE payment system.
Vendors found facilitating cash transactions will be suspended.

## 7. Product Listings
- All listings must be accurate and not misleading.
- Products ₦10,000 and above automatically support Ajo purchase option.
- Vendors may configure custom Ajo settings for their products.
- Prohibited items policy applies to all listings.

## 8. Vendor Upgrade
Vendors may request type upgrades through the platform.
Upgrades require:
- Eligibility verification
- KYC re-verification (if required)
- Admin approval
- Role and permission updates

## 9. Suspension & Termination
DUNAZOE may suspend or terminate vendor accounts for:
- Fraud or misrepresentation
- Multiple unresolved disputes
- Violation of no-cash policy
- Prohibited item listings
- Failure to maintain KYC compliance
